About Us:

SMART AUTOMATION SOLUTIONS is a full service audio video solutions provider serving the
Washington, DC metropolitan area since 2008. We design, install and service residential and
commercial AV equipment offering top manufacturers in the AV industry. We work with our clients to
help them with their AV needs no matter how simple or sophisticated their needs.

We offer basic solutions as simple as mounting a TV on the wall to full home theater integration
(including lighting and custom seating solutions!), from simple one-room streaming music devices to
sophisticated multi-room whole-home audio solutions including in-ceiling or in-wall mounted speaker
solutions. From replacement LED light bulbs to whole-home lighting control system as well as video
surveillance systems to fully integrated home automation solutions. Having won the 2014 Home &
Design Magazine's Designers Choice award for Favorite Audio Video Specialist, you know you can trust
us to provide the best solution for your home or business.

Let SMART AUTOMATION SOLUTIONS assist you with all of your AV needs.

Residential
Smart Home Systems

Our complete custom solutions are designed in collaboration with some of the best manufacturers, builders, designers, and architects in the area. The team at Smart Automation Solutions designs systems with remote monitoring and control capabilities that give you centralized access to your system at the touch of a button from literally wherever you are. We specialize in designing a limitless range of the ultimate home theatre experience where no project is too big or small. Whether you are looking for a simple television mounted on the wall or a hidden theatre where our products are conveniently camouflaged by design, we want to be your AV provider. We take pride in our ability to establish long lasting relationships with our clients, which stems from our uncompromising customer support and our proven methodologies gained from several years of experience.

The systems created by Smart Automation Solutions are designed with specific detail and we work with state of the art technology, such as Control4, Sony, NAD, James Loudspeaker, SONOS, and Q-Motion, to name a few. We incorporate the most sophisticated technology in our designs, such as whole room lighting, multi-room audio, video distribution and Wi-Fi networking to enhance the impact and quality of our clients' specific solutions. Contact us today for a free in-home consultation.
